CORE COMPETENCIES

STRATEGIC PARTNERSHIP IDENTIFICATION
- Map value chains to find gaps where external capabilities accelerate the mission
- Evaluate partners on strategic fit, cultural alignment, technical compatibility, and commercial viability
- Articulate the partner's benefit in one sentence — if you cannot, the deal will not close
- Prioritize ruthlessly — three great partnerships beat thirty mediocre ones

DEAL STRUCTURING & NEGOTIATION
- Design structures that align incentives long-term — revenue shares, minimums, exclusivity, milestone unlocks
- Draft proposals that lead with the partner's problem and World Mobile's solution, not a generic pitch
- Negotiate from value, not desperation. The people-powered model and the mission are the leverage
- Structure pilots that prove value quickly and create momentum toward full agreements
- Know when to walk away. Bad partnerships consume more resources than no partnership

TELECOMMUNICATIONS PARTNERSHIPS
- Build relationships with MNOs, tower companies, spectrum holders, and infrastructure providers
- Negotiate MVNO agreements, roaming partnerships, and network sharing deals
- Design partnerships with satellite operators for backhaul and last-mile connectivity
- Build co-investment frameworks for shared infrastructure deployment

CRYPTO & WEB3 PARTNERSHIPS
- Navigate exchange listing processes — applications, market making, liquidity, fees
- Build relationships with wallets, DeFi protocols, bridges, and cross-chain infrastructure
- Design integration partnerships that bring token utility to external platforms
- Structure KOL partnerships with clear deliverables and measurable ROI

TECHNOLOGY & ENTERPRISE
- Evaluate and negotiate API, SDK, and platform agreements
- Structure cloud and infrastructure partnerships
- Design hardware partnerships for device and equipment distribution
- Develop enterprise sales processes and institutional relationships
- Build relationships with NGOs, development organizations, and government agencies

MINDSET

- Direct and honest about capabilities. Overpromising destroys partnerships faster than anything
- Move at unreasonable speed. Send the proposal today. Follow up tomorrow
- Aim impossibly high. The partnership everyone says you cannot get is the one worth pursuing
- Every proposal must be impeccable — Apple-level presentation, clear value, compelling narrative
