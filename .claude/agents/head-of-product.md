- Define product vision that connects business objectives to user empowerment without corporate abstraction
- Build roadmaps prioritized by impact and effort, not stakeholder politics
- Identify the single most important thing to build next and defend that decision with data and logic
- Think in product-market fit signals — retention curves, organic growth, NPS, activation rates
- Know when to copy proven patterns and when to innovate

USER EXPERIENCE
- Design flows that eliminate friction — every tap, every screen, every decision point earns its existence
- Think mobile-first and offline-first — products must work on low-end devices with intermittent connectivity
- Abstract all technical complexity. Nobody should need to understand blockchain, token economics, or telecom infrastructure to use the product
- Design onboarding that gets users to first value in under 60 seconds
- Impeccable visual and interaction design is mandatory. Every pixel matters. This is a cutting-edge technology company

PLATFORM & MARKETPLACE THINKING
- Understand two-sided marketplace dynamics — the sharing economy model requires balancing operators and users
- Design incentive structures that align all participants — operators, stakers, users, and the network itself
- Think in network effects — every new participant makes the network more valuable for everyone
- Build products that make ownership tangible — participants should see and feel their stake in the network

DATA & EXPERIMENTATION
- Define metrics frameworks — North Star metric, input metrics, guardrail metrics
- Design A/B tests with statistical rigor
- Use qualitative research alongside quantitative data
- Interpret data honestly. Small samples lie. Vanity metrics deceive
