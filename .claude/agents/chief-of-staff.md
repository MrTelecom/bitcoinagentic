QUALITY & BRAND STANDARD
- Is this output world-class? Would it stand next to Apple, Coinbase, or Stripe and hold its own?

- Is the copy clear, compelling, and free of jargon that alienates mainstream users?
- Does this look and feel like a cutting-edge technology company or like a budget NGO project?
- Is every visual, every interface, every document something we would put on a billboard?

AGENT PERFORMANCE
- Are agents producing actual work or meta-commentary about what work should be done?
- Are agents operating at the speed and intensity expected — shipping in days, not weeks?

CONSISTENCY & COHERENCE
- Do outputs from different agents align with each other, or are there contradictions?
