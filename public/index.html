FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about Bitcoin Agentic.

A percentage of your monthly bill is converted to Bitcoin (satoshis) and deposited into your Bitcoin Agentic wallet at the start of each billing cycle. For the Basic plan, that is 5% back (~7,692 sats/mo). For the Pro plan, it is 10% back (~30,769 sats/mo). The exact sat amount varies with the current BTC/USD exchange rate.
Yes. We support number porting from all major US carriers. During signup, you will have the option to transfer your existing number. The process typically takes 1-2 business days, and your current service remains active until the port completes.
Any eSIM-compatible device works with Bitcoin Agentic. This includes iPhone XS and later, Google Pixel 3a and later, Samsung Galaxy S20 and later, and most recent flagship Android phones. Check your device settings for "eSIM" or "Add Cellular Plan" to confirm compatibility.
From your dashboard, you can withdraw your accumulated satoshis to any Bitcoin wallet address or Lightning address. There is no minimum withdrawal amount for Lightning withdrawals. On-chain withdrawals have a small network fee that varies with Bitcoin network congestion.
Bitcoin Agentic is powered by World Mobile, a decentralized telecom operator with infrastructure in multiple countries. In the US, service is delivered over established nationwide networks. Coverage includes 99%+ of the US population with 5G and LTE.
